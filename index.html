About Me

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my portfolio.

I’m a Berlin-based Frontend Developer (React, Next.js, Tailwind) and Graphic Designer, passionate about crafting fast, elegant, and accessible web experiences. As a freelancer, I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate with both startups and established companies around the world, contributing to a wide range of projects from branding and UI design to complex web applications.

My background in graphic design gives me a strong eye for aesthetics and usability, which I combine with solid technical skills in React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy creating intuitive interfaces that not only look great but also provide smooth user experiences. Previously, I worked as a front-end developer at JFX, contributing to high-profile projects like Banco de Chile and Banco Estado. My work includes everything from wireframing and prototyping to front-end implementation and testing.

I'm constantly learning new tools and technologies and eager to take on challenges that help me grow as a developer and designer.
