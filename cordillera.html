FLORERIA CORDILLERA

CATEGORIA Branding Design UX/UI
COMPANY Floreria Cordillera
YEAR 2023
SOFTWARE PS- Indesign

Florería Cordillera is one of the prominent florists in Chile known for its quality flowers and creative arrangements. With over 25 years of experience, Florería Cordillera has established itself as a trusted source for floral needs in the country.

The chosen concept was Delicacy; delicacy in floral designs can be a conscious choice to convey emotions, create elegant atmospheres, adapt to certain occasions, and capture the subtle beauty of flowers and nature.

The logo was crafted based on the concept of delicacy, where we aimed to include a flower replacing the letter 'o'. We presented the logo to the client on both a white and black background, with color variations and in grayscale tones.

We chose pastel tones as the main colors for Florería Cordillera. It was a strategic decision to convey softness, femininity, romanticism, versatility, and visual differentiation in the market

The logo application to test the visual aesthetics of the logo in various visual contexts.Testing the logo on different backgrounds serves to ensure that it functions effectively, is visually appealing, legible, versatile, and maintains visual coherence, all of which contribute to establishing a solid and recognizable brand. Additionally, we can verify its adaptability to a variety of situations and usage contexts. This process helps in assessing whether the logo retains its impact and integrity across various mediums, ensuring its efficacy in different scenarios and maintaining a consistent brand identity.

We test the logo in different formats, such as on the company shirt. This helps us evaluate how it looks and feels in a real-world usage context. It's crucial to ensure that the logo can adapt to various formats without losing legibility or visual quality. Materials and textures can influence how the company is perceived and displayed

The card with the printed logo is a tangible tool that visually represents the brand identity. It's an opportunity to showcase how the logo will appear in a physical format and how it will integrate into the real-world environment of interaction with customers. Cards are items that customers can hold, keep, and share with others. By presenting the logo on a card, a tactile experience is created for customers, reinforcing their emotional connection with the brand and facilitating recognition and recall
