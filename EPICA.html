Epica

CATEGORIA Branding Design UX/UI
COMPANY Epica
YEAR 2023
SOFTWARE PS, Illustrator

Epica is a well-known brand in Chile, recognized for its high-quality products and unique designs. However, as the market evolved, they realized the need to revamp their image to better convey the concept they wanted to transmit. The brand sought to maintain its reputation for excellence while appealing to a younger, more modern audience. To achieve this, EpicA embarked on a rebranding journey, focusing on creating a fresh and contemporary identity that resonated with their target demographic.

In addition to the image overhaul, Epica also aimed to launch a new collection and required an impactful marketing campaign to promote it. The campaign needed to capture the essence of the brand's evolution and showcase the innovative designs and superior craftsmanship of their latest products.

As part of the branding design for Epica, the collection catalog features sleek and layouts that showcase the brand's latest products in a visually appealing manner. The chosen color palette for the brand reflects sophistication and elegance, with a combination of deep navy blue, gold accents, and crisp white to convey a sense of luxury and timelessness.

Epica maintains an active presence on social media platforms, providing updates on new collections, promotions, and events, allowing clients to stay informed about the latest developments from the brand.

The main logo is a refined and versatile design, while the negative version provides a striking contrast for various applications.

The brand's history, mission, and vision are communicated through compelling storytelling on the website, emphasizing their dedication to creating timeless pieces that embody sophistication and style. Epica's design concepts cater to discerning clients who appreciate luxury and exclusivity.

Instagram is a widely used social media platform by Epica to connect with their audience. Through engaging posts, dynamic stories, and interactive content, the company utilizes Instagram to promote their products, share relevant news, and keep their followers engaged. With an effective marketing strategy on Instagram, Epica Negocio Chileno has managed to build a strong community and increase their online visibility.

Through strategic planning and creative execution, Epice successfully redefined its brand image, infusing it with a sense of sophistication and modernity while staying true to its core values. The new collection campaign was launched with great success, generating excitement and interest among both existing and potential customers. As a result of these efforts, Epica solidified its position as a leading fashion brand in Chile, appealing to a wider audience and setting the stage for continued growth and success in the future
