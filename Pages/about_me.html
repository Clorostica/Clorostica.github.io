About Me

Currently, Berlin is my home, where I've been involved in a wide range of projects, from corporate branding to web design. My main focus is on capturing the essence of each project through captivating visuals, always striving to deliver impactful and meaningful results.

I'm excited to share my passion and expertise, working together to create exceptional outcomes. For more details, feel free to visit my website. Over the years, I have developed products for companies and businesses worldwide, ranging from marketing websites to complex solutions and enterprise applications, always with a strong emphasis on delivering fast, elegant, and accessible user experiences.

Currently, I work as a freelancer for various small businesses. Prior to this, I held a position as a front-end developer for a startup called JFX, where I contributed to the creation of web pages for Banco de Chile and Banco Estado. In other companies, I served as a web designer, with roles focused on interface development through an interactive design process. This included the creation of sketches, mock-ups, prototypes, and ultimately user interface design.

I constantly seek to learn new software and tools. My objective is to continually push the boundaries of my abilities, learn something new every day, and approach challenges head-on with a solution-driven mindset.
