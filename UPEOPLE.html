U People

CATEGORIA Branding Design UX/UI
COMPANY Itechnik
YEAR 2023
SOFTWARE PS, Illustrator

U People has established itself as one of the leading human resources service companies in Chile and also has a presence in other Latin American countries. This company offers a wide range of services in the human resources field, including personnel recruitment and selection, personnel management, outsourcing, human resources consulting, training and development, among others. U People stands out for its focus on service quality, adaptability to the specific needs of each client, and the use of cutting-edge technology to optimize human resources management processes. Additionally, it has earned a reputation for its commitment to business ethics and compliance with labor regulations.

Colors and Concepts used to create the logo proposal: integrity, ethics, innovation, adaptability, humanity.

Fonts used

Adaptability and closeness, alluding to the quality of the company and its professionals. "Agreement-Union of people" Key concept to understand that the company, together with clients, reach work agreements to improve the different areas to be advised.

We adapt the virtual platform to all kinds of devices, from web browsers and mobile phones to tablets, through responsive design, which enhances the user experience. Google and other search engines value user experience, including accessibility on devices

We adapt the new corporate image to the platforms owned by U People.

Using the new branding design across different elements, such as company mugs, helps maintain brand consistency across all platforms and marketing materials. The mugs with the new branding design serve as an effective way to promote the brand. When employees use these mugs in the office or at events, they are organically promoting the brand.

We focus on human resources agency business cards as a fundamental tool to convey a professional image. In the realm of human resources, where trust and credibility are paramount, a well-designed card helps project an image of professionalism and seriousness to clients, candidates, and business partners
